# Privacy Policy

**Comic Viewer** is a local desktop application. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data.

## Data Collection

Comic Viewer does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

## Data Stored Locally

The application stores the following data **only on your local device**:

- **Reading positions**: The last viewed page per file, saved to `%APPDATA%\comic-viewer\positions.json`

This data never leaves your device and is used solely to restore your reading position when reopening a file.

## Third-Party Services

Comic Viewer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
